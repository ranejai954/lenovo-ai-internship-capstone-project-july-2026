# ⚡ VoltSaver AI
## AI-Powered Energy Saving Advisor

### 1. Why This Problem?
India's electricity bills average ₹3,000-₹5,000 monthly. Most households don't know how to reduce their consumption, leading to wasted energy and money. This affects SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy.

### 2. The Problem:
- No awareness of energy usage patterns
- High electricity bills
- No personalized, actionable saving tips

### 3. My Solution:
VoltSaver AI is an Agentic AI solution that:
1. **Calculates** monthly energy consumption (kWh)
2. **Shows** monthly cost (₹8 per unit)
3. **Gives** 3 personalized energy-saving tips
4. **Shows** potential monthly savings (₹500-₹2,000)

### 4. How It Works:
- Built with **Streamlit** (Python web framework)
- Powered by **Groq Llama 3** (fast, free AI)
- Users describe their appliances
- AI instantly calculates and generates tips

### 5. Technology:
| Component | Tool |
|-----------|------|
| Frontend | Streamlit |
| AI Model | Groq (Llama 3.1) |
| API | Groq API |
| Language | Python |

### 6. Impact:
- 💰 Saves ₹500-₹2,000 per month
- 🌍 Reduces carbon footprint
- 🇮🇳 Makes SDG 7 accessible to all
- 📱 Scalable to millions of Indian households

### 7. SDG Alignment:
**SDG 7: Affordable & Clean Energy**
- Promotes energy efficiency
- Reduces household energy consumption
- Contributes to sustainable development

### 8. Conclusion:
VoltSaver AI demonstrates how Agentic AI can solve real-world problems - helping people save money while contributing to a sustainable future.
